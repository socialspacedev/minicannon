The experimental music festival <a href="https://www.linesofflight.co.nz/" title="Official Lines of Flight website" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Lines of Flight</a> has been running for 25 years in Dunedin. It's a premier event lauded internationally attracting performers from all over NZ and the world. I have attended every one except maybe the first in 2000. I also performed in <a href="https://www.pyramidclub.org.nz/2009" title="Link to 2009 LOF poster" target="_blank" rel="noopener">2009</a> under the moniker of <a href="https://tillakaratne.bandcamp.com/" title="Bandcamp page for Tillakaratne" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Tillakaratne</a> with my friend Chris.

I used my trusty Olympus OM 1 and Ilford Delta 3200 for the first time. The film was certainly fast enough for dark gig settings but I had a 50mm lens and no zoom.
